Molar Mass Calculator

    Rules for calculating molar mass

  • To calculate the molar mass of a compound, enter the compound and number of moles needed.
  • Only include parentheses if they are followed by a number. Example: Ca(OH)2
  • No brackets []
  • Correct notation must be used or formula will not parse correctly. Example: Na3AlF6, not NA3ALF6 or na3alf6.
